Privacy Policy

Who We Are and What This Is

Erebbit is a social platform where people can share their thoughts, knowledge and ideas.
Everything related to your privacy goes here. All this data is considered and treated as confidential.

How We Collect Information

There are two kind of information that we get from our users.
1. First kind of information is provided by users directly (email, passwords etc).
2. Second kind of information is not provided but generated by users (comments, points etc).
It's our biggest responsibility to ensure that your information is secured. To accomplish that we encrypt all of your private data by default. Data is also generated by users (subjects, comments, boards) which is publically accessible.

How We Use Information We Collect

Your usage information is mostly used to run the logic of website. Your points and comments are used in different features of this website. We use it to know how well a subject or comment is going and how many people are interacting with it.
Your personal information including your emails are always private. We don't use them in any way other than sending you emails related to your account.

Information You Choose to Display Publicly on the Network

This information is generated by user as he uses our site. Subjects, comments, boards, points etc are included in this information. This information is public by default.

Information You Give to Us

You provide us with your email, name (including first name and last name), profile picture, your bio etc. Your email, password and personal information is always hidden.
